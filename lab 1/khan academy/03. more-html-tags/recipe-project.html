Recipe #1 - Besh

  • Time: 2 h
  • Serves: 10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Horse Meat 5 kg
Flour 0.5 kg
Eggs 2
Water 8 liters

Step 1: Boil meat with salt & onion.

Put your meat and 1/2 the onion in a deep enough dish to cover it with cold water leaving enough space on the top so that it doesn't boil over. Bring to the boil, constantly removing the foam, Reduce heat to simmer when it starts to boil. Cover it.Let it cook for about 2 1/2 hours or until the meat is tender and falling off the bone.

Step 2: Prepare your noodles.

In a bowl mix the sifted flour (300-400 g), the eggs (whisk the eggs before you add into flour), add salt and pour water until a dough is formed. Knead pastry, adding flour or water as needed. Knead the dough well, wrap in plastic wrap and leave for 20-30 minutes. Sprinkle the work top with sifted flour and divide your dough into a few small balls Then keeping the worktop lightly covered with flour roll each piece of pastry into a fairly thin layer.

Step 3: Prepare for serving.

Remove the meat and bones, separate as you will only serve the tender cooked meat. cook the noodles in batches in the same stock for about 7-8 minutes. Sieve them out on to the big plate leaving the space in the center for meat. Then add your meat chopped in bite size pieces and put it in the center of the dish. Pour some broth over the meat.

Serve and enjoy the beautiful Besh!

Source: International Cuising website "KAZAKH BESHBARMAK (BOILED MEAT WITH NOODLES)"

Recipe #2 - Lagman

  • Time: 1 h
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Meat 500 g
Noodles 500 g
Water 8 liters

Step 1: Prepare the meat sauce.

Fry the meat with vegetables and spices to get a sauce.

Step 2: Prepare the noodles.

Boil the noodles for 20 mins until soft.

Step 3: Put the lagman up.

Add noodles and the meat together to get the lagman.

Serve and enjoy the beautiful Lagman!

Recipe #3 - Plov

  • Time: 1 h
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 1 kg
Meat 500 g
Water 8 liters
Carrots 2 pieces

Step 1: Prepare the meat.

Fry the meat with carrots until carrots are soft. Then add water, salt and boil for 20 minutes.

Step 2: Add the rice.

Wash the rice twice and add it to the boiling carrots and meat. Boil on a strong heat until the water almost is all used. Then close the lid and let it boil for 5-10 minutes.

Serve and enjoy the beautiful Plov!
